Build Better Habits!

While it takes about 66 days to form a habit, most people don’t realize how close they are to success because they don’t track their progress. This simple app accomplishes that by providing a clear visual of your journey, showing you exactly how close you are to the finish line.

Total = How many total times you have completed a goal

Streak = How many continuous days you have completed a goal

Reset Every (days) = Your streak resets if you miss this many days.
